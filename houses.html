Indian Homes: Traditional and Modern Styles

Kutcha House

A kutcha house is a basic, temporary dwelling in rural areas.

Duplex House

A duplex house is a single building split into two separate living units with their own entrances.

Haveli

A haveli is a traditional, ornate, mansion-like residence in India, often featuring large balconies.

Town House

A townhouse is a multi-floor, single-family home that shares one or two walls with neighboring units.

Palace

A palace is a grand, luxurious, and massive official residence for a sovereign, head of state, or other high-ranking noble.

Cottage

A cottage is a small, cozy, old-fashioned, or rustic house, often located in a rural area or used as a vacation or summer home.

Pent House

A penthouse is a luxurious apartment located on the highest floor of an apartment building.

Villa

A villa is a large, luxurious, detached house or resort residence set on its own private grounds, often featuring a garden or pool.

Farm House

A farmhouse is a residential dwelling historically attached to a farmand a location in a rural or agricultural setting.

Bungalow

A bungalow is a single-story, small house or cottage with a low-pitched roof and often a wide front porch, designed for simple, accessible living.
